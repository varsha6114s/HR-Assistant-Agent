# Company HR Policies and Guidelines

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of annual leave per year
- Annual leave accrues at 2 days per month
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance for approval
- Annual leave is pro-rated for new joiners based on their start date

### Sick Leave
- Employees receive 12 days of paid sick leave per year
- Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year
- Medical certificate required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave can be taken in half-day increments

### Casual Leave
- 8 days of casual leave per year
- Can be taken without prior approval for emergencies
- Maximum 2 consecutive days at a time
- Cannot be combined with other leave types

### Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave
- Must be taken within 8 weeks before and after delivery
- Can be extended by 4 weeks unpaid if required
- Requires medical documentation

### Paternity Leave
- Male employees receive 2 weeks of paid paternity leave
- Must be taken within 6 months of child's birth
- Requires birth certificate submission

## Working Hours

### Standard Hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Lunch break: 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM (1 hour)
- Total working hours: 40 hours per week

### Flexible Working
- Core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (mandatory presence)
- Flexible start time: 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM
- Flexible end time: 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM
- Work from home available 2 days per week with manager approval

### Overtime
- Overtime paid at 1.5x regular hourly rate
- Must be pre-approved by manager
- Compensatory off available instead of payment if preferred

## Benefits

### Health Insurance
- Comprehensive health insurance for employee and family
- Coverage: Employee, spouse, and up to 2 children
- Annual coverage limit: ₹5,00,000
- Cashless treatment at network hospitals
- Dental and vision coverage included

### Provident Fund
- Company contributes 12% of basic salary to EPF
- Employee contribution: 12% of basic salary
- Voluntary PF contribution allowed

### Performance Bonus
- Annual performance bonus up to 20% of annual salary
- Based on individual and company performance
- Paid in April each year

### Professional Development
- Annual training budget: ₹50,000 per employee
- Conference attendance supported
- Online course subscriptions provided
- Certification exam fees reimbursed

### Transportation
- Company shuttle service from major locations
- Fuel reimbursement for personal vehicle: ₹3,000/month
- Parking facility available

## Code of Conduct

### Professional Behavior
- Maintain respectful workplace environment
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Confidentiality of company information mandatory
- Conflict of interest must be disclosed

### Dress Code
- Business casual attire Monday to Thursday
- Casual Friday (jeans allowed)
- Formal attire for client meetings
- Company ID badge must be visible

### Remote Work Policy
- Available for eligible roles
- Requires manager approval
- Must maintain availability during core hours
- Company laptop and VPN access provided
- Home office setup allowance: ₹15,000 (one-time)

## Grievance Redressal

### Process
1. Raise concern with immediate manager
2. If unresolved, escalate to HR within 7 days
3. HR will investigate within 15 days
4. Final decision communicated in writing
5. Appeal to management committee if needed

## Resignation and Notice Period

### Notice Period
- Employees: 60 days notice required
- Company: 60 days notice or payment in lieu
- Probation period: 30 days notice

### Exit Process
- Submit resignation letter to manager and HR
- Complete exit interview
- Return all company property
- Handover responsibilities
- Full and final settlement within 45 days
