# Employee Benefits Guide

## Overview
This guide provides detailed information about all employee benefits offered by the company.

## Health and Wellness

### Medical Insurance
- **Coverage Amount**: ₹5,00,000 per family per year
- **Family Definition**: Employee + Spouse + 2 dependent children
- **Network Hospitals**: 5000+ hospitals across India
- **Cashless Facility**: Available at all network hospitals
- **Reimbursement**: Claims processed within 15 days
- **Pre-existing Conditions**: Covered after 2 years
- **Maternity Coverage**: ₹75,000 per delivery
- **Annual Health Checkup**: Free for employee and spouse

### Dental and Vision
- **Dental Coverage**: ₹25,000 per year
- **Vision Coverage**: ₹15,000 per year (glasses/lenses)
- **Preventive Care**: 100% covered

### Mental Health Support
- **Counseling Sessions**: 6 free sessions per year
- **24/7 Helpline**: Available for all employees
- **Stress Management**: Workshops conducted quarterly
- **Meditation App**: Free subscription to Headspace

### Gym Membership
- **Reimbursement**: Up to ₹2,000 per month
- **Corporate Tie-ups**: Discounted rates at partner gyms
- **Yoga Classes**: Free weekly sessions at office

## Financial Benefits

### Provident Fund (PF)
- **Employee Contribution**: 12% of basic salary
- **Employer Contribution**: 12% of basic salary
- **Voluntary PF**: Additional contribution allowed
- **Interest Rate**: As per government norms (~8.15%)

### Gratuity
- **Eligibility**: After 5 years of service
- **Calculation**: 15 days salary for each completed year
- **Maximum**: As per Payment of Gratuity Act

### Employee Stock Options (ESOP)
- **Eligibility**: After 1 year of service
- **Vesting Period**: 4 years (25% per year)
- **Exercise Window**: 10 years from grant date

### Performance Bonus
- **Annual Bonus**: Up to 20% of annual CTC
- **Criteria**: Individual performance + Company performance
- **Payment**: April of following year
- **Pro-rated**: For employees joining mid-year

### Referral Bonus
- **Amount**: ₹25,000 per successful referral
- **Payment**: After candidate completes 6 months
- **Unlimited**: No cap on number of referrals

## Work-Life Balance

### Flexible Working Hours
- **Core Hours**: 10 AM to 4 PM (mandatory)
- **Flexible Start**: 8 AM to 10 AM
- **Flexible End**: 5 PM to 7 PM
- **No Fixed Desk**: Hot-desking available

### Work From Home
- **Frequency**: 2 days per week
- **Approval**: Manager discretion

- **Equipment**: Laptop, monitor, accessories provided

### Sabbatical Leave
- **Eligibility**: After 5 years of service
- **Duration**: Up to 3 months unpaid
- **Frequency**: Once every 5 years
- **Approval**: Requires senior management approval

2. Manager approval
